Writing for the future—A brief introduction

2023-12-09 2 min read # WRITING

ur world is changing, technologies revolutionizing our world in only a few decades. At the moment, millions of factories and machines are raging, manufacturing all kinds of necessities; thousands of scientists are being submerged in the lab, rigorously assessing the new techniques. Humankind is walking to unity, to prosperity, to the bright future we all expected.

Everything seems going well, yeah? BUT the truth is, something are currently going wrong. Pollution, climate change, diseases, and famine, destroy our world, reaching out their claws to millions of millions of people. Instead of ignoring it, the better choice is to save those lives by developing environmentally friendly techniques, introducing hybrid wheat and rice, and inventing specific medicine of those dreadful COVID.

Technologies are being used to reimagine and reshape our world, but they should not be done at the expense of lives and felicity. As a teenager, writing simply became the most appropriate mean of influence and expression. This is a website for me to practice writing, post my writing, and influence the world. I will post my thoughts, feelings, and writings irregularly on the website. Some ideas you may like, others you may not. All in all, hope you enjoy!
